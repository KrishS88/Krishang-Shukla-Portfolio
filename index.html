Projects and Experience

Below are some of the extracurricular, organizational, and project-based work I have undergone since high school to the present. Hover over each image to learn more!

The Bangalore Lakes Documentary Project. During my residence in Bengaluru, I noticed immense pollution in the city's famous lakes. I filmed and edited a documentary that can spread awareness of the issue and turn to the many solutions we have for the community. The documentary can be viewed here.
Using an modeling extension called A-frame, I designed a 3D scene in which a user can move the camera around the scene. The goal was to turn a 2D image into a 3D scene with depth, and my choice was to convert a silhouette of Gotham City into a 3D environment. You can view the scene here.
The Young American Patriots Fife and Drum Corps is a marching band centered around Revolutionary War themes and classical instruments. I was a part of this band for over six years. I was also promoted to Drum Sergeant and later the President of the organization. We conducted numerous performances to major events, including Universal Studios Hollywood and the International Grand Prix races held at Laguna Seca. You can learn more about YAPS here.
Sri Surabhi Go Ksetra is a non-profit organization of Sustainable Eco Alliance committed to a clean and protected environment for the natural grazing of cows and soil fertility. I was in charge of minor fundraisers and a manager of the sanctuary maintenance. You can learn about the organization here.
I worked with a team during my Computational Media coursework to develop a fully functional AI LLM Chatbot. We designed this chatbot to be themed around the popular character Wednesday Addams. With a custom profile of her face and overall dark palette, users can enjoy conversations as if they were speaking to Wednesday herself. The program can be found here.
Another major project in my Computational Media coursework was to develop a website game. Our team made a time-based matching card game using JavaScript, HTML, and React called "Yellow Jacket Match-It". The game features a stopwatch and a turn counter to help you record your best gameplay. You can play the game here.
I applied to the University of Michigan's miRcore annual programming events for medical research. For both 2021 and 2022 sessions, I worked with a team to find trends and patterns in DNA mutations using a database oriented programming language, R.
My local temple started a program called "Sandwhich Seva", where a group assembled to prepare bagged lunches to those in need. During the pandemic, this program was shut down, so me and my sister opened a new branch of Sandwhich Seva at home. Together we would prepare over a hundred bagged lunches and deliver them to the Tri-Valley Haven food pantry once a week from 2020 and 2022.
